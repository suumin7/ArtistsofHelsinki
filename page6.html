Name: Liisa Tuulikki

Art Style: Designer, Ceramicist and Painter

Liisa Tuulikki: a story of inspiration

Liisa's journey into the world of art began in the picturesque landscape of her coastal hometown in Finland, where she grew up surrounded by the gentle rhythms of the sea and the vibrant colors of nature. As the youngest of four siblings, Liisa found herself captivated by the world of creativity from a tender age.

"I was always drawn to art," Liisa reminisces with a smile. "From doodling on scraps of paper to creating intricate drawings inspired by the beauty of our surroundings, art was my constant companion."

Her artistic journey took a decisive turn when she applied to a specialized art high school in Helsinki. Venturing away from her close-knit community, Liisa found herself immersed in a world where creativity knew no bounds.

"The decision to attend the art high school was a pivotal moment for me," Liisa reflects. "It was there that I truly began to understand the depth and breadth of artistic expression. Surrounded by like-minded individuals and inspiring mentors, I felt a sense of belonging and purpose."

University offered Liisa the opportunity to further explore her artistic interests, eventually leading her to discover her passion for ceramics and glasswork. Despite the challenges inherent in mastering these mediums, Liisa remained undeterred in her pursuit of artistic excellence.

"Ceramics spoke to me in a way that no other medium had before," Liisa explains. "There was something inherently magical about shaping raw clay into beautiful, functional pieces of art. With each piece, I found myself delving deeper into the intricate dance between form and function."

Drawing inspiration from a myriad of sources, including anime culture and pop art, Liisa began to develop her distinctive artistic style—a vibrant tapestry of colors, shapes, and textures that reflected her unique perspective on the world.

"My art is a reflection of who I am—a blend of my personal experiences, interests, and passions," Liisa shares. "Through my work, I strive to evoke emotions, spark conversations, and create moments of connection with those who encounter it."

Despite the inevitable obstacles encountered along the way, from navigating the complexities of setting up her own studio to managing the business aspects of her art career, Liisa remained steadfast in her determination to succeed.

"Every challenge has been an opportunity for growth," Liisa affirms. "Whether it's overcoming technical hurdles in the studio or navigating the intricacies of the art market, I've learned to approach each obstacle with resilience and determination."

Looking to the future, Liisa dreams of expanding her artistic horizons, showcasing her work on an international stage, and leaving a lasting legacy through her art.

"I want my art to transcend borders and boundaries—to inspire, uplift, and bring joy to people from all walks of life," Liisa declares. "For me, art is not just a vocation; it's a calling—a journey of self-discovery and creative exploration. And I'm excited to see where it takes me next."
