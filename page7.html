Melissa Tran: a story of inspiration

Mel, also known as "Bonded by Mel," didn't inherit her passion for nail art from family tradition. Instead, she discovered her knack for intricate designs during her formative years while waiting for her mom, a nail technician, to finish work. It was here, in the vibrant world of nail art, that Mel's journey as an artist began.

While many teens were navigating high school, Mel was honing her craft, delving into the world of nail design during her free time. Initially viewed as a backup plan, her interest in nail art quickly blossomed into a thriving side hustle, thanks to her mother's guidance and her own dedication.

As Mel's skills grew, so did her confidence. Opening her Instagram account marked a turning point, as she began attracting clients beyond her immediate circle. Through trial and error, Mel refined her artistic style, drawing inspiration from fellow nail artists and the diverse array of designs found on platforms like Pinterest.

But success didn't come without its challenges. Early mishaps with the drill taught Mel the importance of precision and control while balancing her burgeoning nail business with her academic commitments required careful planning and prioritization.

Despite the obstacles, Mel's perseverance paid off. She found fulfillment in cultivating a loyal clientele and witnessing the transformation her art brought to others. Through her work, Mel aims not only to boost her clients' confidence but also to express their unique personalities through intricate nail designs.

Looking ahead, Mel dreams of expanding her social media presence, collaborating with fellow nail artists, and continuing to inspire others with her creations. Ultimately, she hopes to leave a legacy of empowerment, showing others that confidence can be found at the tip of a brush.
